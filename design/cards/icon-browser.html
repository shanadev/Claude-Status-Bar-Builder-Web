Behavior

dataicons.json fetched lazily on first open (nerd-fonts glyphnames + emoji-test + CLDR annotations; skin tones excluded); grid is virtualized — thousands of cells stay smooth
searchmatches glyph name, NF prefix (nf-md-, nf-fa-…), CLDR keywords; "/" focuses, arrows navigate the grid, enter = use
selectsingle click selects (green ring) and fills the footer detail row; double-click or "Use icon" commits and closes
fallback notechoosing an NF glyph surfaces a one-line hint: "needs a Nerd Font in your terminal" — amber, dismissible, remembered
recenta "recent" filter chip appears once anything has been used; last 24, stored with the theme
dismissEsc or ✕ closes without committing; backdrop is var(--gib-void) at 75% with no blur (cheap on WASM)
