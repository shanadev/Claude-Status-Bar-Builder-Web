Color tokens

Blue-black glass surfaces, phosphor-tinted text, seven neons. Prefix --gib-* (Full Gibson). These become app.css custom properties verbatim in Phase 2.

Voids & surfaces

--gib-void#04060apage backdrop, behind the grid floor
--gib-bg#0a1016panel background
--gib-surface#0f1720raised cards, inputs
--gib-line#1e2c3chairline borders, dividers

Neons

--gib-green#00ff66ok / success / phosphor flavor text
--gib-cyan#00e5ffinteractive: links, focus, selection
--gib-magenta#ff2ea6secondary accent, section headers
--gib-amber#ffb300warnings, retro amber moments
--gib-red#ff3b4derrors, destructive, intrusion alert
--gib-violet#8b5cfftertiary, gradient partner
--gib-claude#ff8a5cbrand bridge: Claude terracotta, neonized
