The hero. A real xterm.js instance dressed as a CRT monitor — bezel, vignette, scanlines, one soft roll bar. The whole costume is a toggle: "crt fx" off strips it to a bare terminal for a clean look at the raw line. What renders here is byte-identical to what the renderer emits in a real terminal. Below the bezel: sample-data sliders that exercise thresholds live.
